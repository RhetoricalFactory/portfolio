Herbal Medicine: Adaptogens

Did you know?

Adaptogens are a category of healing herbs characterized by their ability to perform well with other medications and supplements. They help the body adapt to stress on many levels. Stress can be emotional, physical and mental. Adaptogens apply their healing qualities wherever the body needs support. Herbal medicine has been cataloged throughout the ages, with physical records uncovered as early as 1500 BCE. Most prescription medications have been derived from plants, and their latin or botanical names will indicate such.
